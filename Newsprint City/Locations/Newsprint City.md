#newsprint-city #sets-locations 
**Newsprint City** is the city in which the fan-story of *Splatoon Eternal Thunderstorm* takes place. It is based on, and a direct evolution of, New York City.

Many locations that are directly derived from important locations before the Great Flood. However, some locations, such as [[Olympia Tower]], are not derivations of New York locations, but are simply inspired by New York locations.
