#fe-lake-of-mist 
The **Hoshi no Tama** is the PRF weapon of [[FE Lake of Mist/Characters/Seolyeong|Seolyeong]]. It starts in [[Huyao]]'s possession, and he theoretically could use it, but he warns that "he's never been good with magic" (which is true, as his Magic stat is 0 and its growth is 5%), and he will not use it in Chapter 0.
