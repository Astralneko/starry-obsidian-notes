#fe-lake-of-mist #newbeglia
The **Caliphate of Newbeglia** is a nation visited briefly in the primary arc of [[Part 2 of Lake of Mist]]. As it is where the titular lake is located, it also serves a primary role in [[Part 3 of Lake of Mist]].
## History
Newbeglia is formerly a democratic state with a constitutional monarchy. However, the monarch took control of the legislature in a recent crisis, and it has yet to revert.
