#fe-lake-of-mist #argonia
The Holy Empire of Argonia is the "main antagonist" force of Lake of Mist, as several, but not all, of the main antagonists are from Argonia.
## Relations
Argonia is the most obvious anti-laguz force in Ranselia. The official policy is to not treat them as persons. Although this is obviously not practiced by everyone in Argonia, most in Argonia will hold negative stereotypes of laguz.
