Debazi is the national language of the [[Republic of Debaz]]. A lot of this information is on the wiki, but information can be written and messed around with here, before going onto the wiki.
## Phonology
Debazi has a bunch of consonants and a fair few vowels, though the exact counts of each depend on the analysis. See [[Debazi phonology]] and [[Dialects of Debazi]] for more details.
## Orthography
Debazi is written natively with a logography-abjad hybrid, and also has a romanization and arabicization. See [[Debazi orthography]] for more details.
